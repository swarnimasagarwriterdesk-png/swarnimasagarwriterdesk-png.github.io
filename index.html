The Author

Swarnima Sagar is a banker by profession and a writer by choice. Her writing explores love, memory, and emotional silences across generations. Beyond technical and professional journey, her heart has always resonated with rhythm, words, and emotions — and through them, a different version of her emerged: a Writer. Her writing expresses feelings beyond digits and logic, weaving together emotion and imagination into poetry, stories, and screenplays.
